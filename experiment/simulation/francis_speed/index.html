FRANCIS TURBINE - Constant Speed

Objective

To conduct the performance test on Francis turbine under constant speed.

Apparatus used:

Francis Turbine setup with Centrifugal Pump, Pressure Gauges, Weighing Balance with Weights, etc.


 Description Francis turbine: is a type of water turbine. It is an inward-flow reaction turbine that combines radial and axial flow concepts.

Cavitation: is a phenomenon which manifests itself in the pitting of the metallic surfaces of turbine parts because of the formation of cavities.
